About Us

The Eclipse IoT Working Group is a collaboration between organizations and individuals who share the goal of creating an open IoT. The collaboration focuses on the development, promotion and adoption of open source IoT technology. Many of our members provide a wide range of projects and services built on top of Eclipse IoT technology.

Membership benefits

  • Members can participate in the working group meetings, including technical and requirements discussions.

  • Members can participate in developer outreach programs, including webinars, surveys and events.

More information

More information about the IoT WG is available on our wiki page and feel free to join our mailing list. Email us if you have questions.
