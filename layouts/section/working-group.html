Join Us

The pace of IoT innovation is faster than ever. Open source has won because no single company can compete with the scale and speed of disruptive innovation delivered by collaborative ecosystems. The Eclipse IoT Working Group provides a forum for individuals and organizations to build and promote the open source software, standards, and collaboration models needed to create a scalable open Internet of Things.

With nearly 3 million lines of code across over 35 projects, supported by over 40 of the world’s leading IoT ecosystem players, Eclipse IoT is the open source center of gravity for IoT.

Join your industry peers to shape the open IoT ecosystem!

A Proven Track Record

Here are just some of the reasons why Eclipse IoT is trusted by industry leaders:

  • Eclipse IoT has been very successful in promoting open IoT projects and attracting new developers and users to IoT technologies. Our members gain adoption of their projects, which in turn helps with promoting their commercial products.
  • The Eclipse Foundation’s clear, vendor-neutral rules for intellectual property sharing and decision-making make it easy to collaborate with other organizations on driving rapid innovation.
  • The Foundation’s legal and intellectual property processes enable members to fully comply with antitrust laws and provide members with the legal assurance that they can successfully embed open source technology into their commercial products.
  • The Eclipse Development Process is a set of best practices that guide member organizations during the creation of open source projects.

Membership Benefits

Members of the Eclipse IoT Working Group take advantage of the following opportunities:
  • Participate in industry collaborations to develop common open IoT platforms for Industrial IoT, Industry 4.0, Smart Home, Edge Computing, and more.
  • Play a role in defining Eclipse IoT strategic priorities
  • Gain insights into the Eclipse IoT technology roadmap and direction
  • Ensure a valuable career path for IoT developers and experts
  • Benchmark and learn best practices from peers for leveraging open IoT technologies to accelerate product development and improve time-to-revenue
  • Promote commercial support and services they may provide for Eclipse IoT technologies
  • Promote the value of open IoT ecosystems through testbeds
  • Promote IoT cloud interoperability through open standards (e.g. OneM2M)
  • Partner with Eclipse IoT member companies on IoT research projects
As an Eclipse IoT member, you also gain access to the following Eclipse community benefits:
  • Marketing programs that target the Eclipse developer community, including advertising on iot.eclipse.org, preferential listing on Eclipse Marketplace, exposure for commercial products on Eclipse Marketplace Client, organizing Eclipse Demo Camps, plus other programs.
  • Discounts on sponsorship and registration fees for Eclipse Foundation events, including EclipseCon Europe.
  • Networking opportunities with other member organizations through Foundation run events and one-on-one introductions by Foundation staff.
  • The ability to use the Eclipse Foundation member logo on your own site and have your logo listed on our members’ page and in other promotional material.
